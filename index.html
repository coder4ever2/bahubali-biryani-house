Our Story

Spices, Soul
& Stories

Nestled in the heart of Downtown Vancouver at 1130 West Pender Street, Bahubali Biryani House has been the city's most authentic Indian dining destination since 2017.

Our chefs bring time-honored recipes from Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh, and across India — from slow-cooked Dum Biryanis to sizzling Tandoor platters, every bite is a journey home.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday in our 60-seat lounge, enjoying our famous Lunch Buffet, or having dinner delivered to your door — the warmth of India awaits you.
